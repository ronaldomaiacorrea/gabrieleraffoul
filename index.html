About me

My name is Gabriele Raffoul. I graduated from West End Academy and have 4 years of experience in the field of aesthetic medicine and non-medical aesthetics. I am Brazilian from the city of Cruzeiro SP, and even before I graduated from the aesthetic school, I already did certain treatments on friends and family.

I do this work with great affection and dedication with my clients. My specialization is exactly Brazilian wax, because the name itself already says a lot about the origin of this service, and really in Brazil this type of hair removal is very popular and the most requested by people. All my products and materials are natural and recyclable of first quality. Made in a clean and calm environment. I will be available for any questions that may arise.
